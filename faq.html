Grant Programs

Our company tries our hardest to provide free services to those who need it. Check below to see if you qualify.

Apply For Grants Here

What does the CWP include?

  • Free website development, domain names, SSL, business emails & hosting.
  • Free logo design, free graphic design 

To qualify for the Community Web Program (CWP) you will need to fulfil one of these requirements.

  •  Small Business with less than three employees or less than 10kprofit (after wages and bills).
  •  Charity Or Non Profit with at least 50% of income donated.
  • A school or classroom leader
  • Community Centers

Please reach out if you would like to apply or ask questions.

What does the SAP include?

  • Free portfolio websites or community engagement websites.
  • Free domain names and hosting

In order to qualify for the SAP free services, you must be a student currently enrolled in a high school or college course or have recently left/graduated. 

Part of the SAP includes free and discounted computers, laptops, phones and software for low-income high school or college students. Low income must always be proven. Reach out to our team for more information.

To qualify for the CHP, you must fulfil all of the following requirements.

  • A small business who also qualifies for the CWP
  • A currently or recent student
  • A school or charity
  • Individuals who have a disability

What does CHP include?

  • CHP includes free hardware repair, setup installation
  • CHP includes free hardware at request (limited grants).
